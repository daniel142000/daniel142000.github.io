Mission Statement

I am currently pursuing my Dream of becoming one of the Greatest Filmmakers to walk this earth, and I have seen where Jamaica has failed to benefit from such a flourishing industry. I am going to become the first Jamaican to be Awarded with an Oscar for best Director, best Screenplay and Best Picture. My Ultimate Goal is to Open very Own Intentionally know Film Production Company, in which where I Want the Head Quarters to be here in Jamaica. This would lead to the films being distributed from Jamaican even if they were shot in foreign countries. And Most Importantly this would provide job opportunities for the people out here that share the same unique talent of Animation and Motion picture. People wouldn't see migration as their best way to be accommodated, accepted and even being paid for their talent. And ultimately Jamaica would be put on the map as a Film Capital.
